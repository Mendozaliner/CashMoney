## What we learned about investing philosophies
- **Trend following (v2)** continues to be the most robust approach: it avoids both
  the dot-com and GFC drawdowns (SMA200 gate), scales risk sensibly (vol target),
  and earns T-bill yield on cash. The philosophy works because markets trend more
  than random-walk theory predicts.
- **Momentum crashes** are real and significant. Pure momentum (E3, E5) needs a
  trend gate or quality filter to avoid buying into the final stage of bubbles.
- **Defensive assets aren't always safe**: TLT's -46% draw in 2022 broke dual
  momentum's bond-as-safe-haven assumption. In an inflationary/rising-rate regime,
  no simple 60/40 or trend-follow-to-bonds approach works as expected.
- **Kill-switch DD management**: The concept is sound (LTCM teaches us survivorship
  matters) but parameters need to respect the strategy's natural DD range. Triggering
  inside the normal volatility zone creates whipsaw without reducing ruin risk.
