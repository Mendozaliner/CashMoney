## Keep/revert decision
KEEP E3 as champion v2. Rule check: OOS Sharpe 0.967 > 0.876 ✓; MaxDD improved
(-17.9% vs -20.8%) ✓. Components each independently beat v1 OOS (E1: 0.914,
E2: 0.925), and E2's parameter neighbors also beat v1 (0.920-0.939) —
the improvement is a plateau, not a spike. Turnover stays trivial (~2/yr).
Honest caveats: (a) tv=0.18 is a grid-edge cell — as tv→∞ E2 degenerates to
v1, so the marginal vol-scaling benefit needs a wider grid check (roadmap);
(b) combining E1+E2 was a design choice, not OOS selection, but the composite
was confirmed on OOS — treat v2's edge over its components as unproven;
(c) rf-cash gain is partly mechanical (T-bills paid ~4-5% in 2022-25 flat
periods) and is regime-dependent.
